Classic Board Games > Battleship

Battleship is a strategy type guessing game for two players. It is played on ruled grids (paper or board) on which each player's fleet of warships are marked. The locations of the fleets are concealed from the other player. Both players take turns on guessing where eachothers ships are placed, the first to sink all their opponents ships win.
